22 Feb 2011 on design

Creating seamless, directionally-tiled images with Gimp

Don't bother with the default Make Seamless filter. You want the resynthesizer plugin (gimp-resynthesizer under APT).

I used the plugin, along with the Edge-Detect -> Neon filter and some manual editing, to construct a new banner image for this site out of one of my favourite xkcd comics. The colour scheme kind of reminds me of orange chocolate; I hope it looks sensible to people who aren't me :)
